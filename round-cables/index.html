Round Cables for Industrial Applications by Gimpex Cables

Uncompromised Power for Unforgiving Environments

In every industrial environment, the flow of uninterrupted power defines success. From CNC machines and heavy-duty power tools to high-capacity generators and factory automation systems, performance starts with the right cabling infrastructure. That is where Gimpex Round Cables come into play, engineered with precision, built to last and trusted across critical applications.

Designed specifically for industrial usage, Gimpex Round Cables provide a stable, durable and efficient power supply solution for machinery, workshops and energy-intensive tools. Whether in high-temperature zones, oil-laden environments or rugged outdoor conditions, our round cables are crafted to perform, without compromise.

Engineered for Heavy-Duty Industrial Applications

With decades of experience in the electrical and cable manufacturing sector, Gimpex Cables understands the operational demands faced in modern factories, workshops and manufacturing units. Our round cables are manufactured using top-grade copper conductors, double insulation layering and a toughened outer sheath for enhanced resistance against wear, vibration and chemical exposure.

These cables are ideal for supplying power to:

  • Industrial equipment and plant machinery

  • Heavy-duty hand tools and power systems

  • Conveyor systems and production line automation

  • Generator and transformer connections

  • Portable machinery and control cabinets

Each cable is designed to handle mechanical stress, resist voltage fluctuations and provide safety across a range of voltages and operating conditions.

What Sets Gimpex Round Cables Apart?

Gimpex round electrical cables are more than just wires, they are critical tools that ensure uninterrupted power transmission for sensitive and power-hungry applications. Here's why professionals across industries choose us for their power cabling needs:

  • High Conductivity with Pure Copper Strands: At the heart of every Gimpex Round Cable lies high-purity, annealed copper conductors that ensure superior conductivity and minimum energy loss. The strands are designed for flexibility, allowing easy installation even in tight, curved pathways without risking internal damage.

  • Advanced Insulation for Maximum Safety: To guarantee electrical integrity, each conductor is individually insulated with premium-grade PVC insulation, which acts as a barrier against short circuits, overheating and current leakage. The materials used are selected for their high dielectric strength and thermal endurance.

  • Robust Outer Sheath for Industrial Durability: Our round cables are reinforced with a thick, abrasion-resistant PVC sheath that protects against oils, greases, chemicals, mechanical impact and UV exposure. This makes them ideal for environments where durability and external protection are not optional but essential.

  • Stable Power Transmission in Every Scenario: Designed to deliver consistent voltage and uninterrupted power, our round cables support efficient energy transfer to motors, drives and heavy tools, reducing the risk of downtime due to voltage drop or conductor fatigue.

  • Flexible Design for Easy Routing: Despite their rugged build, the cables offer excellent flexibility, enabling efficient routing through cable trays, conduits and compact electrical panels. This reduces strain during installation and allows better fitment in space-constrained environments.
