Premium Solar DC Cables by Gimpex Cables

Powering the Future of Renewable Energy

As solar energy becomes the cornerstone of sustainable power, every component in a photovoltaic system must perform flawlessly. Among these, Solar DC Cables play a vital role in connecting solar panels to inverters and charge controllers, ensuring that energy flows smoothly with minimal loss. Gimpex understands the critical nature of these connections and delivers Solar DC Cables designed to optimize energy transfer, resist environmental stresses and stand the test of time.

With over two decades of expertise in electrical solutions, Gimpex brings precision engineering, strict quality control and advanced material technology to every meter of Solar DC Cable. The result is a cable solution that not only meets stringent national and international standards but also delivers peace of mind for residential, commercial and industrial solar projects.

Why Solar DC Cables Are Essential?

In solar power installations, where electrical efficiency directly impacts return on investment, the quality of the cabling cannot be compromised. The cables act as the backbone of the system, transmitting direct current (DC) from solar panels to inverters with minimal power loss. Any compromise in material, insulation or construction can result in reduced efficiency, safety risks or long-term degradation.

Gimpex Solar DC Cables are specially designed to withstand harsh outdoor conditions while ensuring excellent conductivity, resistance to mechanical stress and stable electrical performance. Whether used in rooftop installations, solar farms or hybrid off-grid systems, our Solar Cables provide the reliability and power integrity that every solar setup demands.

Key Features That Drive Performance

When powering clean energy, quality is non-negotiable. Here’s why professionals across India trust Gimpex for their solar installations:

  • High Current Capacity with Low Transmission Loss: Constructed with finely stranded, high-purity copper conductors, our cables ensure superior conductivity. This reduces energy loss and supports high current loads, enabling more efficient transmission between solar modules and inverters.

  • UV and Weather Resistant Insulation: Built for long-term outdoor use, our Solar DC Cables come with double-layered insulation made from UV-stabilized, halogen-free compounds. This offers outstanding resistance against sunlight, rain, dust and fluctuating temperatures, ensuring optimal performance in extreme weather conditions across North India.

  • Flame Retardant and Halogen-Free: Safety is central to every product we manufacture. Our solar cables are flame retardant and halogen-free, reducing toxic emissions in case of fire and making them safer for use in residential, commercial and industrial environments.

  • Tangle-Free Flexibility and Easy Installation: The cables are designed with flexibility in mind, making routing through conduits, ducts and tight spaces effortless. Even in low-temperature conditions or high-altitude installations, they retain pliability without cracking or losing form.

  • Long Service Life: With excellent thermal and mechanical strength, Gimpex Solar DC Cables maintain electrical integrity over prolonged exposure to outdoor elements. Our cables are built to last, ensuring stable output and minimal maintenance over years of continuous operation.

Applications of Gimpex Solar DC Cables

Our cables are trusted across a wide range of solar energy installations, including:

  • Photovoltaic panel to combiner box connections

  • String inverters and central inverter setups

  • Battery bank and off-grid solar system wiring

  • Rooftop solar installations for homes and offices

  • Large-scale solar farms and solar parks

Every solar project, whether residential or industrial, benefits from efficient cabling. With our in-house quality control processes and vertically integrated manufacturing, Gimpex ensures that every batch meets stringent technical benchmarks before it reaches the end user.

Available Specifications

Gimpex Solar DC Cables are available in single-core and multi-core variants, with standard conductor sizes ranging from 1.5 sq. mm to 35 sq. mm. Both red and black colour options are offered to aid polarity identification. Custom lengths and packaging are also available on request for bulk or project-based requirements.

With strong partnerships with regional stockists and authorized dealers, we ensure timely deliveries and continuous stock availability for projects of every scale.
