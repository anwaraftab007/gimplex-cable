Gimpex Cables provides an extensive range of electrical wires and cables tailored for various applications across residential, agricultural, industrial and commercial sectors.

Twin Flat Cables: Twin flat cables are used in domestic and commercial low-voltage circuits, offering consistent current flow and easy installation. Their robust design ensures reliability in concealed and surface wiring setups.

CCTV Cables: For secure and reliable video surveillance, CCTV cables ensure smooth transmission between cameras and monitoring devices. They perform effectively in both indoor and outdoor security setups.

Speaker Wires: For sound systems requiring superior audio clarity, speaker wires offer pure signal transmission and enhanced sound quality for home theaters, auditoriums and public address systems.
