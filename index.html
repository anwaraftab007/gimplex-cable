Company Introduction

Powering Connections with Trust & Technology

Gimpex Cables has been energizing India’s growth since 2004 with safe, durable and high-performance wiring solutions. From homes to industries, agriculture to infrastructure, our cables are designed to deliver unmatched reliability and strength. With a state-of-the-art in-house PVC unit and over 20 lakh meters annual wire production, we ensure consistent quality and large-scale supply without compromise.

Built on decades of expertise and innovation, we continue to power progress with products that guarantee safety, efficiency and performance in every connection.

  • Over 20 years of industry trust & expertise
  • Wide product range for home, industry & agriculture

  • Quality you can trust, performance you can rely on
