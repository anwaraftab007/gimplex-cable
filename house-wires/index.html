Premium House Wires for Safe and Efficient Electrical Fittings

Trusted Electrical Wiring Solutions for Homes and Commercial Spaces

When it comes to setting the foundation for any electrical system, the choice of house wire plays a critical role. At Gimpex Cables, we design and manufacture premium-quality house wires built to meet the highest safety standards and performance benchmarks. Whether you're wiring a residential apartment, a retail outlet or an office space, our house wires ensure stable power flow, dependable insulation and maximum protection against overloads or electrical faults.

With over two decades of expertise in the electrical manufacturing sector, Gimpex Cables has become a preferred name across North India for quality-focused wiring solutions. Every meter of our house wire is crafted for seamless installation, longevity and complete peace of mind.

Powering Modern Living with Confidence

House wires are not just carriers of power; they are protectors of life and property. Poor-quality wires can overheat, cause short circuits, damage equipment or even start fires. In contrast, superior-quality house wires provide secure, stable electrical performance, safeguarding both people and investments.

Gimpex house wires are manufactured using high-grade copper and special-grade PVC insulation that ensures smooth conductivity, thermal resistance and enhanced electrical safety. From ceiling fans and lights to sockets and switches, our wires form the foundation of everyday functionality in modern spaces. Their flexibility, strength and superior insulation make them ideal for concealed wiring and open wiring in homes, offices, retail spaces, educational institutions and healthcare facilities.

Key Advantages of Gimpex House Wires

When performance, safety and durability matter, our wires deliver outstanding value. Here’s what sets them apart:

  • High Conductivity Copper: We use specially drawn, bright-annealed copper conductors that ensure low resistance and high conductivity. This enables efficient electricity transmission while minimizing energy loss. Better conductivity also means less heat generation and greater safety.

  • FR and FRLS Insulation Options: Our house wires are available with Flame Retardant (FR) and Flame Retardant Low Smoke (FRLS) insulation variants. These materials provide excellent resistance to fire, delay flame propagation and significantly reduce toxic smoke in the event of a short circuit or fire, giving occupants more time to react.

  • Smooth Installation Experience: Flexibility and uniform thickness of the insulation make our wires ideal for quick and hassle-free installation in concealed and surface-mounted electrical layouts. They are easy to strip, bend and connect, making work faster for electricians and safer for end users.

  • Resistance to Moisture and Chemicals: Gimpex house wires are designed to perform under real-world conditions. Our insulation resists moisture, oil, grease and mild chemicals, making them suitable for both household applications and semi-commercial installations where exposure to varying environments is expected.

  • Long-Lasting Performance: Engineered with high-quality PVC compounds and tested under multiple stress conditions, our wires are built to last. They resist aging, remain pliable and continue to deliver consistent performance even after years of installation.

Applications of Gimpex House Wires

Our house wires are suitable for a wide range of electrical installations including:

  • Internal wiring in residential buildings

  • Power distribution in shops and offices

  • Wiring for lighting and general electrical fittings

  • Connection of domestic appliances and control panels

  • Wiring in small industrial units and institutional buildings

Whether it’s concealed wiring behind walls or open wiring in commercial areas, Gimpex House Wires deliver consistent performance across all setups.

Colours and Sizes to Match Every Need

Gimpex House Wires are available in standard colours like Red, Yellow, Blue, Green and Black for easy phase identification and safer installations. Our wires come in multiple sizes such as 1.0 sq. mm, 1.5 sq. mm, 2.5 sq. mm, 4.0 sq. mm and higher to meet the diverse load requirements of your electrical layout.

Each coil is packaged to ensure convenience, handling safety and longevity. Available in 90-meter standard coils and custom lengths on request.
