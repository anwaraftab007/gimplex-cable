Our History

Our Journey Through Time

Gimpex Wires & Cables began its journey in 2004 in Delhi with a vision to empower India’s electrical future through quality-driven solutions. The initiative was founded by the enterprising Bansal Brothers, Raman and Atul, who started with a trading setup that quickly earned trust for its integrity and dependable service.

With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, the brand advanced in 2005 by establishing its own modern manufacturing unit. This strategic step transformed the venture from a trading background into a respected name in electrical manufacturing.

From the very beginning, the focus has been on delivering reliability, innovation and craftsmanship. What started as a modest initiative has grown into a brand that continuously strengthens electrical infrastructure across regions, supplying products trusted by households, industries and agricultural sectors alike. The history of Gimpex Cables reflects not only growth but also an enduring promise to power progress with safe and efficient wiring solutions.

Mission

Director’s Message

Welcome to Gimpex Cables Industries. From the very beginning, our vision has been to deliver trusted electrical solutions that bring safety, reliability and efficiency to every household, business and infrastructure project.

What started as a small initiative has today grown into a recognized name across North India, with a strong presence in Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, Assam, Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Uttar Pradesh. Our journey has been guided by a clear focus on uncompromising quality, customer trust and ethical practices that ensure steady growth and lasting relationships.

At Gimpex Cables, every product is manufactured under strict ISI-certified processes to ensure lasting performance and safety. Our diverse range of wires and cables is designed to meet the needs of modern living and industry, from everyday household requirements to demanding commercial and industrial applications. Each solution reflects our focus on innovation, durability and environmental care.

Unlike many brands that depend on third-party suppliers, Gimpex produces its PVC insulation in-house. This gives us complete control over quality and consistency, resulting in insulation that is flame-retardant, durable and resistant to moisture and chemicals. By maintaining this process internally, we deliver superior protection and reliability in every wire we produce.

As we look ahead, our promise is to continue investing in advanced technology, expanding our distribution network and building strong partnerships with the electricians, engineers, retailers and end-users who place their trust in us. With Gimpex Cables, you choose durability, dependability and progress that stand the test of time.
