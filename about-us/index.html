Driven by Quality. Powered by Trust.

Established in 2004 and headquartered in Delhi, Gimpex Wires & Cables, a brand of Gimpex Cable Industries, has been driving India’s electrical infrastructure with reliable, high-performance wiring solutions. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to deliver dependable wiring solutions with uncompromising quality.Founded by the enterprising Bansal Brothers — Raman and Atul, the establishment started as a trading firm and quickly earned a reputation for trust and service.

Fuelled by a strong focus on quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, we took a decisive step forward in 2005 by establishing our own state-of-the-art manufacturing unit. This strategic move marked the beginning of our evolution from a trading business into a respected and quality-driven manufacturer in the electrical industry.

Delivering Excellence Across Industries

With over two decades of hands-on experience, Gimpex has become a trusted name in the wire and cable manufacturing sector. Our extensive product portfolio meets the diverse needs of the agricultural, residential, commercial and industrial markets. We combine time-tested expertise with modern technology to deliver cables that are built to last, safe to use and tailored for performance.

Supporting our commitment to quality and scale, our manufacturing facility includes an advanced in-house PVC production unit with an annual capacity of 2,000 tons, andwire production capacity of 20 lakh meters. This ensures we consistently meet large-scale demands without compromising on performance or reliability.

Our Product Range

We manufacture a comprehensive range of wires and cables, designed to meet modern electrical demands: Submersible Flat Cables, Aluminium Wires, Round Cables, House Wires, Coaxial Wires, Cat6 Cables, Flexible Wires, Twin Flat Cables, Solar DC Cables, CCTV Wires, and Speaker Wires.

Designed to withstand challenging environments, our cables support energy efficiency, long-term safety and seamless performance across all applications.

Director’s Message

Welcome to Gimpex Cables Industries. From the very beginning, our vision has been to deliver trusted electrical solutions that bring safety, reliability and efficiency to every household, business and infrastructure project.

What started as a small initiative has today grown into a recognized name across North India, with a strong presence in Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh. Our journey has been guided by a clear focus on uncompromising quality, customer trust and ethical practices that ensure steady growth and lasting relationships.

At Gimpex Cables, every product is manufactured under strict ISI-certified processes to ensure lasting performance and safety. Our diverse range of wires and cables is designed to meet the needs of modern living and industry, from everyday household requirements to demanding commercial and industrial applications. Each solution reflects our focus on innovation, durability and environmental care.

Unlike many brands that depend on third-party suppliers, Gimpex produces its PVC insulation in-house. This gives us complete control over quality and consistency, resulting in insulation that is flame-retardant, durable and resistant to moisture and chemicals. By maintaining this process internally, we deliver superior protection and reliability in every wire we produce.

As we look ahead, our promise is to continue investing in advanced technology, expanding our distribution network and building strong partnerships with the electricians, engineers, retailers and end-users who place their trust in us. With Gimpex Cables, you choose durability, dependability and progress that stand the test of time.
