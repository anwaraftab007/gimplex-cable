Premium Flexible Wires by Gimpex Cables

Built for Adaptability. Designed for Long-Term Safety.

Electrical infrastructure is only as strong as the wires that run through it. At Gimpex Cables, our flexible wires are designed with precision engineering and manufactured using superior-grade materials to offer high conductivity, exceptional durability and unmatched ease of use across a wide range of electrical installations.

Whether you're wiring appliances, designing control panels or working on complex industrial circuits, Gimpex Flexible Wires adapt to your layout and perform consistently even under dynamic conditions. From residential to commercial and industrial applications, they are the reliable choice for professionals who demand flexibility without compromise.

With over two decades of leadership in the wire and cable sector, Gimpex continues to drive innovation in electrical connectivity through products that combine safety, quality and versatility.

Why Flexible Wires Are Essential in Modern Electrical Setups?

Modern electrical environments demand more than just standard wiring solutions. With increasing automation, compact layouts and space constraints, flexibility in wiring is no longer optional. Rigid wires often fail under repetitive movement or tight routing, leading to maintenance issues and reduced lifespan.

Gimpex Flexible Wires are made using high-purity copper conductors in a multi-strand configuration, allowing seamless routing through tight spaces without stress fractures or conductivity loss. The advanced-grade PVC insulation resists heat, oil, moisture and abrasions, ensuring dependable performance in high-demand environments.

Key Features of Gimpex Flexible Wires

Every wire manufactured at Gimpex Cables is a result of stringent quality control, tested to perform under real-world stress conditions. Here is what sets our flexible wires apart:

  • High Conductivity Pure Copper: Each wire is manufactured using 99.97% pure electrolytic grade copper, ensuring low resistance and superior electrical performance. This translates to minimal energy loss and efficient power usage in every application.

  • Multi-Strand Flexibility: Our flexible wires are built using finely drawn copper strands twisted together for maximum flexibility. This construction offers greater resistance to metal fatigue and supports easier cable routing during installations.

  • Flame-Retardant PVC Insulation: The outer sheath is made from high-quality flame-retardant PVC, providing not just insulation but also protection against heat buildup, short circuits and potential fire hazards.

  • Enhanced Durability and Surface Finish: Gimpex Flexible Wires feature a smooth outer finish that resists abrasion and is easy to handle during installation. The wires are moisture resistant, making them suitable for indoor and semi-outdoor environments prone to occasional humidity.

  • Vibrant Colour Coding for Safer Installations: To support organized electrical layouts and simplify identification, our flexible wires are available in standardised colours suitable for live, neutral and earth connections. This aids in phase identification and reduces the risk of error during maintenance.

Applications of Gimpex Flexible Wires

Versatility is the hallmark of our flexible wires. Their ability to bend without compromising on conductivity makes them ideal for:

  • Appliance Wiring: Ideal for internal connections in household appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines, fans and air conditioners.

  • Control Panels: Perfect for complex control units where space is limited and wiring flexibility is crucial.

  • Automotive and Machinery Wiring: Used in vehicles and industrial equipment where vibration resistance and adaptability are required.

  • Lighting Installations: Suitable for decorative lighting, LED setups and indoor-outdoor lighting projects.

  • Residential Wiring: Provides safe and efficient wiring solutions for homes, ensuring protection and durability.

  • Temporary and Portable Connections: Useful in exhibitions, construction sites and setups that demand frequent movement or re-routing of wires.

No matter the complexity or environment, our flexible wires deliver consistent performance, easy handling and long-term reliability.
